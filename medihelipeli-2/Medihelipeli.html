How to win

The idea for the game is to rescue all the Antidote Carriers with the immunity from different cities and return them to the main hospital to make an antidote to the virus.

For every returned patient you gain more range to save more Antidote Carriers.

The helicopter has a maximum capacity of 3 Antidote Carriers.

The game ends when you have rescued all the Antidote Carriers or you ran out of range.

There is an epidemic outbreak across the world and the virus have landed to cold and dark Norway. The scientists of Norway have discovered that some of the humans are immune to the virus. In the Trondheim main laboratory in Norway, they believe that it is possible to make an antidote from the blood of those humans.

There has been rumors going around that there is 12 humans alive who have this immunity around the Norway. Because of the epidemic you are the last medical helicopter pilot who is capable to rescue those humans.

If you manage to rescue every patient before the fuel runs out, you'll be hailed as a hero of Norway. Otherwise, the rest of the human race faces an uncertain fate.
